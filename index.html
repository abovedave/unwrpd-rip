Wrapping up Unwrpd

2017-2021

When we started unwrpd, we set out with the mission to make the plastic-free, zero-waste lifestyle as convenient as possible. We said that if you could ever buy a bamboo toothbrush on the high street, then our job was done.

After 5 years, hundreds of subscriptions and thousands of products unwrpd.com has shipped it’s last order. From 23 March 2020 we will no longer ship subscriptions.

Building unwrpd has been our way to contribute what we could to shaping the future of conscious consumption. Being zero waste or plastic-free isn’t about being perfect, it’s about making small changes that add up to a big difference.

Now more than ever, we passionately believe that as consumers the most powerful way to vote is with our wallets. Every pound, dollar, and euro you spend is a vote for the world you want to live in.

We want to express a huge thank you to our customers and suppliers for taking this journey with us.

However, this isn’t the end of the story.

The spiritual successor to unwrpd is Beagle, a project we have both contributed to bringing into existence as we continue the fight for conscious consumption. It’s a simple way for you to shop more sustainably and ethically.

See you around!
